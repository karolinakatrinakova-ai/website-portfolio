Privacy

A short, plain privacy note

This is a small portfolio site. I don't run analytics, ads or any tracking. Your visit isn't recorded anywhere.

What your browser stores

Two tiny things, kept only in your browser: your chosen language (EN, SK or CS), and your answer to the cookie banner. That's all — nothing is sent to me or anyone else.

When you write to me

If you email me or send the contact form, your message arrives in my inbox. I use it only to reply. I don't share it with anyone and I don't sign you up to any newsletter.

Your rights

Email me at karolina.katrinakova@gmail.com any time and I'll tell you what (if anything) I have stored about you, or delete it.
