About

Hi, I'm Karolína.

I'm a designer-developer with a real-world marketing background. FAQ

A few things you might be wondering

I already have a domain and an old website. Can you do something with that?

Yes. We can either move the domain to a new website or redesign on top of what you have — depends on the setup. We'll figure it out together on the first call.

What if I don't have any photos?

For the first version we can use beautiful free photos from Unsplash — the same kind you see across this website. Later, when you're ready, we replace them with your own.

Will I be able to make small edits myself?

For simple things like text, prices and photos — yes. I'll show you how. For bigger changes, just write to me.

Do you only work for the kinds of businesses shown here?

Not at all. The work above is just what I have ready to show. If you run a small business and you'd like a website, write to me and we'll see if it's a fit.

If you're thinking about a website, write to me.

No pressure, no commitment. Just a short conversation about what you'd like to make.
