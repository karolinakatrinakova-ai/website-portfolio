FAQ

You often ask

I already have a domain and an old website. Can you do something with that?

Yes. Either I migrate the domain to a new website, or I redesign on your existing host. Depends on the tech — usually I can migrate without any downtime.

What photos do you use if I don't have any?

For the first version I use styled photos from Unsplash (legal, free). We replace them with yours later. On higher tiers I can arrange a photographer at a fair price.

Will I be able to make edits myself?

On Pro and Plus tiers — yes. I set it up so editing texts, prices or gallery is doable in a simple interface. Or write me and I'll handle it under the monthly care plan.

What if I don't like the result?

Before we start I send a clear offer with references. After the first preview you have 1–2 included revision rounds. If we really miss the mark, I refund 50% of the deposit and we part on good terms.

Do you also work for businesses outside the templates shown here?

Absolutely. The 6 templates are demos for the most common asks (salons, cafés, clinics, photographers, florists, yoga studios) but I've also built sites for tutors, mechanics, bakeries, real estate agents, B&Bs and wedding planners. The templates are starting points, not limitations.

What's the monthly check-up?

€25/month. I do small content edits (new prices, photos, opening hours), keep things up to date, monitor uptime, run a monthly performance report and stay on WhatsApp for quick questions. Cancel any time.
