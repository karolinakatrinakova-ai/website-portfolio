Features

What your website can include.

Pick what your business actually needs, skip the rest. The website itself is custom-coded — no Wix, no WordPress, no heavy templates. For payments, bookings and emails I plug in proven third-party platforms — picked per project — so your data stays safe and things just work.

Online booking & payment

Booking system with calendar, capacity and cancellation rules. Card payment, automatic email confirmations. Through a trusted booking platform — or a custom solution like in the Aria demo.

Online shop & cart

Products, variants, delivery zones, cart and checkout. Through a trusted e-commerce platform — chosen by scale and needs. Like in the Stem florist demo.

Weekly schedule

A weekly schedule grid you update yourself. Clients see what's free and filter by class, teacher or room. Try it on Aria.

3+ languages

One click switches the entire site. Perfect for tourist towns, B&Bs, photographers, border-region businesses.

Galleries & lookbooks

Filterable, full-screen, before/after — whatever shows your work best. See Rove (photo) and Lumi (salon).

Customer accounts

Login, booking history, passes and credits. Clients come back more often when the site remembers them.

Menus & reservations

Tasting menus, daily specials, allergens, 3-step reservations. See Sála (restaurant) and Luna (café).

Card payment & passes

Card payment through a trusted, secure payment provider. Single visits, multi-class passes, gift cards, subscriptions.

Maps & directions

Live Google Maps, opening hours, parking notes, public-transport stops, click-to-call.

Blog / journal

Easy-to-update articles for SEO and brand voice. You write in plain text, the site stays beautiful.

Forms & lead capture

Wedding inquiries, contact forms, quote requests — straight to your inbox, with anti-spam.

Reviews & testimonials

Selected reviews shown on your site + a button to your Google reviews. Social proof builds trust faster than anything else.

SEO & analytics

On-page SEO, sitemap, schema, GA4 / Plausible, Search Console — set up properly from day one.

Mobile-first, accessible

90%+ of small-business traffic is mobile. I design for the phone first, then expand. WCAG-friendly.

Emails & newsletter

Contact form → straight to your inbox. Optional newsletter through a trusted email platform — whichever fits you best.

FAQ

A few things you might be wondering.

Are these demos real client websites?

No. They are showcase demos I built from scratch to show what's possible. Real client work stays private. Your site will be entirely your own — different colours, structure, features and voice.

How long does it take?

Most small-business sites go live in 2–4 weeks. Bigger projects with shops, booking systems or memberships take 4–8 weeks. You'll get a clear timeline in the quote.

Will it look like one of the demos above?

No — and that's the point. Every site is designed from a blank canvas around your business. The demos just show what's technically possible (booking, shop, multilingual, schedule, etc.). Yours will be its own thing.

I already have a domain and an old website. Can we keep them?

Yes. We can keep your domain and either redesign on top of what you have or move you to something new — depending on the setup. We'll figure it out on the first call.

What if I don't have any photos yet?

No problem. For the first version we can use beautiful free photos (like the ones on this site). Later, when you're ready, we swap them for your own.

What if my business isn't in the demos?

It probably isn't — there are a million kinds of small businesses and only 7 demos here. Write to me, tell me what you do, and I'll show you sites I'd reference for your sector and what I'd build.

Are there ongoing costs?

Just hosting and domain (around €5–10 / month, paid directly to the providers). No platform fees to me, ever. Optional monthly care if you want a real human on call for edits.
