Pricing

A fair price for a real piece of work.

After our first call you get a written, fixed price. You always know what you're getting and what it costs.

I don't have fixed packages. Some businesses need a single home page; some need five pages with a multilingual toggle and a Google Map block; some want me to write all the copy and source the photos. Pricing depends on what we're actually making.

The way it works:

  1. You write to me with a few words about your business and what you'd like.
  2. We have a short conversation — phone, email, or in person if it makes sense.
  3. I send you a clear, fixed quote in writing. No surprises, no hourly games.
  4. If it's a yes, we start. If it's a no, no harm done.
