Services

What I can help you with.

Strategy, design, code, copy and care — done by me, end to end. No handoffs.

  1. 01

    Strategy & UX

    A look that fits your business — calm, considered, on brand. We sketch ideas together over a couple of conversations and refine until it feels right. No moodboards forced on you, no design by committee.

    Good for: a brand-new website, or a redesign of something tired.

  2. 02

    Custom design & build

    Your website is built from scratch, just for you. No Wix, no Squarespace, no monthly platform fees. It's fast, lightweight, and yours to keep — for good.

    Good for: businesses who want a real website without recurring software fees.

  3. 03

    Copy that converts

    If writing about your own business feels strange, I'll draft the copy for your home page in plain, warm language. I bring a marketing background — I know what tends to land and what tends to feel like noise.

    Good for: people who'd rather not stare at a blank Google Doc.

  4. 04

    Be found locally

    Setting up your Google Maps listing and getting the basics right on your website — so when someone in your area searches for what you offer, you show up. Your address, opening hours and contact details stay consistent everywhere they need to be.

    Good for: businesses where most clients live nearby — cafés, salons, clinics, studios.

  5. 05

    Three languages

    If your customers come from across the border or include English-speaking visitors, I can add a simple language toggle to your site. Same content, three languages, one click. Just like this website.

    Good for: border-region businesses, B&Bs, photographers, anyone in tourism.

  6. 06

    Ongoing care

    If you'd like, I can keep an eye on your website each month — minor edits, fresh photos, content updates, small tweaks. No commitment, no minimum term. We just talk and figure out what's useful.

    Good for: businesses that change often (menus, schedules, prices) and want a real human to ask.

Sound like a fit?

Write to me and we'll see if it makes sense.
