Background

Where I come from

Before Sage Studio, I worked at Kofio — one of the fastest-growing direct-to-consumer coffee brands in Central Europe. I owned the marketing stack end to end, and that's where I learned how a small website really fits into the life of a small business.

Marketing strategy

Quarterly planning, channel mix, customer-acquisition targets. I learned that every page on a website is a marketing decision — not just a design choice.

Social-media content

Instagram, TikTok and LinkedIn — strategy, content calendar, on-brand visual systems, copy that doesn't sound like a press release.

Campaign management

Meta Ads, Google Ads, influencer collaborations, email sequences. Reading dashboards every morning, killing what doesn't work.

Business analytics

Reading the numbers and translating them into next steps — what to change on the website, what to write, what to drop.

Team management

Briefing freelancers, running content sprints, keeping things on track. It taught me how to communicate clearly with non-technical people — which is most of you.

AI workflows

Using AI tools for content drafts, brainstorming, and quiet automations. I bring practical AI helpers into projects only where they save real time — never as a gimmick.

What I do

Honest, simple websites

Here's what I offer — and what I don't pretend to offer.

The website

  • A custom design — sketched together, refined until it feels right
  • Hand-coded — no Wix, no Squarespace, no monthly platform tax
  • Responsive — looks right on phone, tablet, and desktop
  • Multilingual ready — EN + SK + CS toggle if you need it
  • Local-business essentials — Google Map listing, simple SEO basics
  • You own everything — your domain, your content, your hosting

Optional monthly check-ups

  • Small content edits — new prices, photos, opening hours
  • Light SEO tuning — small content tweaks, keyword updates
  • A real human to ask — quick questions, no ticket system
  • No commitment — start any time, stop any time
How I work

What I do — and what I don't

✓

I tell you straight

If your idea isn't quite there, I'll say so. If it is, I'll show you how to make it stronger.

✓

Fixed quotes

Once we agree on what we're making, you'll know what it costs. No hourly games, no surprise invoices.

✓

I take time

I'd rather build one website with care than three websites in a hurry. We'll set a realistic timeline together.

✗

No platform lock-in

Your site lives in your account, on your hosting. If you ever want to leave, you take everything with you.

✗

No upsell game

I won't add a blog, a shop, or three integrations if you don't need them. We do what serves your business.

✗

No agency speak

"Synergy", "pivot", "leverage" — these stay outside the room. We call websites websites.
