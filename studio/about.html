Background

Two years at Kofio shaped how I think about websites

Before Petal Studio, I worked at Kofio — one of the fastest-growing direct-to-consumer coffee brands in Central Europe. I wasn't there to push pixels: I owned the marketing stack end to end.

Marketing strategy

Quarterly planning, channel mix, customer-acquisition cost targets, retention campaigns. I learned that every page on a website is a marketing decision — not a design choice.

Social-media content

Instagram, TikTok and LinkedIn — strategy, content calendar, on-brand visual systems, copy that doesn't sound like a press release. This is exactly what your website needs to feel alive.

📣

Campaign management

Meta Ads, Google Ads, influencer collabs, email sequences. Reading dashboards every morning, killing what doesn't work by Friday. A website that converts pays for the ads that drive it.

Business analytics

GA4, Looker Studio, Meta dashboards, custom SQL — I read the numbers and translate them into next steps. Your website will ship with proper analytics from day one.

Team management

Briefing freelancers, running content sprints, holding people accountable without being a tyrant. It taught me how to communicate clearly with non-technical clients — which is most of you.

AI workflows

Claude, Cursor, Midjourney, internal AI agents for content drafting and customer-support triage. I bake practical AI helpers into client websites where they save real time — never as a gimmick.
