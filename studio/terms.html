2. How a project starts

Every project begins with a written quote that we both agree on. Pricing is discussed individually based on what we're actually building — there are no fixed packages.

3. Payment

Standard split: 50% deposit before work starts, 50% on delivery. For larger projects we can split further. Invoices are due within 14 days.

4. Timeline

The timeline is agreed in writing in your quote and depends on the size of the project. If materials (photos, copy, info) arrive late, the timeline shifts accordingly.

5. Revisions

The agreed quote includes a fair number of revision rounds — typically two. Major rewrites or design overhauls beyond that are billed separately and we'll discuss before doing anything.

Once the project is paid in full, you own the website and the code. I keep the right to show the project in my portfolio (unless we agree otherwise in writing).

I'm not responsible for outages on hosting, domain registrars, or third-party services (Google, Reservio, etc.). The code I deliver is warrantied for 30 days against bugs.

8. If you change your mind

You can cancel before the first preview — in that case 50% of the deposit is refunded. After the first preview, the deposit covers the time already spent and isn't refunded.

We talk first. If we really can't agree, the relevant court in the place of business has jurisdiction.
