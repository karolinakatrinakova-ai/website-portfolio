FAQ

Common questions

I've never done yoga. Where do I start?

Slow flow, yin or pilates. The intro week (€25) is designed for exactly this — try them all in 7 days, see what fits.

Do you provide mats?

Yes. Free mats and props (blocks, straps, bolsters) are available — please bring your own water bottle and a small towel.

Can I pause my membership?

Up to 2 weeks per month, free. For longer pauses (holidays, injury) email us — we always sort it out.

What if a class fills up?

Join the waitlist when booking — when somebody cancels, the system promotes the next person automatically and emails you.

Do you do private sessions?

Yes. €60/hour with any teacher. Useful if you're returning from injury or want to focus on a specific practice. Email us.
