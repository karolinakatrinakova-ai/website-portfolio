About the photographer

Hello, I'm Sofia

I've been photographing for eight years. I started as an assistant to a wedding photographer in Prague, spent a year in Berlin, and have run my own studio in Vinohrady since 2018.

My work is quiet. I look for the moment after the planned moment — when nobody is performing for the camera, but everything is still happening. That, to me, is what a photograph remembers.

I work primarily with natural light, on Sony A7 IV, with 50mm and 85mm prime lenses. Editing in Capture One — soft, warm, with a gentle filmic grain.

— Sofia
