About the salon

A small Prague salon where the focus is on you, your hair and the result

Salón Lumi has been in central Prague since 2018. Behind the brand stands long-standing expertise, an experienced team and a professional environment that shows in every visit.

We specialise in long hair and colouring techniques that grow with you — balayage, foliage, soft melir. No aggressive bleaching, no "see you in a month for correction".

FAQ

Common questions

How far in advance should I book?

For a cut, usually one week. For colour, two weeks. Around Christmas and before summer, plan for three weeks.

Do you offer a consultation before the first visit?

Yes — 15 minutes free, by email or phone. Send photos of your hair and we'll write back whether what you want is achievable.

Where can I park?

Blue zone right outside the salon, or the OC Flora paid garage 5 minutes on foot.

Do you accept card payments?

Yes — cards, cash, Apple Pay and Google Pay.
