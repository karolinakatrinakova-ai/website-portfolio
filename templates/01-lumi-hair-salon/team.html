Salón Lumi/Team

Five stylists, one salon

A small, deliberately quiet team. Each of us specialises in something different — pick the stylist whose work speaks to you, or let us match you at consultation.

01
Top Stylist · Founder

Ema Nováková

Ema founded Lumi in 2018 after seven years between Berlin and Vinohrady. She trained as a Wella Master Colour Expert and was a finalist at BOMTON CUP three years running. Her chair is mostly long hair — long, layered, and lived-in.

She'll always book a fifteen-minute consultation before a first colour and never lifts more than two levels in one session. Her clients tend to come back every six weeks, which is roughly the longest she'll let your bond rest between visits.

02
Colour Specialist

Lucia Veselá

Lucia has been our resident colourist since 2020. She studied under Goldwell colour education in Vienna and runs the salon's correction work — the kind of jobs that take five hours and a strand-test the week before.

Her speciality is grey integration — moving you out of monthly root touch-ups into a tone that grows out gracefully. If you're tired of the maintenance cycle, this is her chair.

03
Bridal & Editorial

Hana Krajčová

Hana came to Lumi in 2021 after four seasons styling for Czech Vogue and a stint with the team that opened Augustine. Her diary is mostly weddings between April and October, and editorial / lookbook commissions in winter.

She always runs a trial — never a day-of mystery — and travels for ceremonies across Czechia and Slovakia. Mood-board call before booking is included in every wedding fee.

04
Junior Stylist

Klára Dušková

Klára finished her apprenticeship at Lumi in 2024 and now runs her own chair every weekday. She trained alongside Ema for two years, holds Olaplex Level 2, and is steadily building a book of repeat clients.

Booking with Klára is twenty percent below senior rates — not because the work is rushed, but because we want her to grow with clients who like her style.

A few recent transformations

Soft balayage · Ema
