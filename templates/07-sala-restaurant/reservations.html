Reservations · Sála Praha

Three steps,
under a minute

A few seats are held back for walk-ins at the bar — but most evenings the dining room books out two weeks ahead. Pick a date, a time and how many of you, and we'll confirm by email.

When would you like to come?

Pick a date in the next 14 days. Sittings begin at 17:30, last seating 21:30 (22:00 on Saturday).

How many of you?

Up to 6 guests online. For 7+ please email us about the Cellar Room — we'll set the long table for you.

A few last details

We use these only to confirm the booking and reach you if anything changes. Allergies and dietary requests welcome — we cook around them.

See you soon at Sála

A confirmation is on its way to your inbox. We hold tables for 15 minutes after the booking time — if you're running late, please give us a quick call so we can keep your seat.
