Our story

Three years in Old Town, one idea.

We opened Stem in 2022 because we couldn't find a flower shop in Bratislava that worked the way we wanted to work — seasonally, locally, and slowly. Most shops sell the same imported tulips year-round. We don't.

Every Monday we drive to growers in Senec and Pezinok. Whatever's beautiful that week is what we tie that week. If a flower can't survive the journey, it doesn't make the bouquet.

Workshops

Learn to tie a bouquet, properly.

2-hour Saturday workshops, max 8 people. We teach the principles — texture, scale, color, line — and you go home with the bouquet you made.
