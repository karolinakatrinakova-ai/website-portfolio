The studio

A small botanical studio in Vinohrady

Stem began as a flower stand at the Sunday market in 2019. Today we work from a small studio on Slezská — five florists, one delivery van, one rule: only flowers we'd put in our own home.

We grow what we can in a quarter-hectare garden in Polabí (sweet pea, garden roses, dahlia, herbs) and source the rest from three Czech growers — Mělník, Polabí, and a small farm in South Moravia.

— Eva, Stem founder
