Where it comes from

Coffee with a name attached

We work with three importers who buy direct from farms in Ethiopia, Kenya and Colombia. Every bag has a producer's name on it — when we know whose hands picked the cherries, we charge what they're actually worth.

Our brunch ingredients come from Polabí (eggs, butter, herbs), Mělník (rhubarb, soft fruit) and Naše farma in Líbeznice (vegetables). The bakery is daily, the menu is short, the leftovers are minimal.

"Slow coffee, honest food, the same baristas every morning."
