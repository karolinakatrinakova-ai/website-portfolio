Café Luna/Visit & reserve

Vinohradská 14 · Praha 2

Reserve a table

Walk-ins welcome on weekdays. Weekend brunch, Friday wine nights and groups of six or more — we'd recommend reserving. We confirm by SMS within an hour during opening hours, otherwise the next morning.

A few useful notes

  • Walk-ins always welcome on weekdays — we hold roughly half the room for the morning crowd.
  • Tables of six or more, please reserve at least 24 hours ahead — we set the long table specially.
  • Brunch reservations run for 90 minutes. We never rush coffee — we just won't seat the same chair twice.
  • Dogs are welcome — there's a water bowl by the door and a treat behind the bar.
  • Two prams fit comfortably; with three or more, please pick a quieter time slot.
  • Working from Luna is fine until 11am on weekdays. Weekends and after 5pm are screen-free.

Getting here

Vinohradská 14, Prague 2

A 2-minute walk from Náměstí Míru (line A), or 6 minutes from Muzeum (lines A & C). Tram 11 stops directly in front of the door.

Metro

Náměstí Míru (A) · 2 min walk · turn left out of the station.

Tram

11, 13 stop directly in front (Italská). Night tram 91 also runs.

Bike

Two racks under the awning. Rekola station 60m east on Vinohradská.
